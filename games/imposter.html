🦎 Chameleon Game

Word Chart Edition

How to Play:
1. Create or join a room
2. Vote on a category together
3. Everyone sees a chart with 20 words
4. One word is highlighted (except for the chameleon!)
5. Everyone must give one hint about the word
6. Once ALL hints are in, voting begins
7. Vote to find the chameleon
8. Caught chameleon gets one final guess for bonus points!
